Non-Profit Membership

No cost for non-profit organizations. Includes access to networking events, community support, and special programs for NGOs. This membership is tailored to help non-profits connect and collaborate with other organizations.

Technology Access: Basic online directory listing.

×

Bronze Membership

Designed for small businesses, this membership offers advertising opportunities, networking sessions, and discounted access to training programs.

Benefits: Basic advertisement placement and member-exclusive offers.

Technology Access: Business profile in our online directory.

×

Silver Membership

This membership includes advanced advertising options, extended training opportunities, and priority invitations to special events. Perfect for businesses looking to scale their presence in the community.

Benefits: Priority advertisement placement, discounts on community events, and training sessions.

Technology Access: Premium business profile and search optimization in our online directory.

×

Gold Membership

The ultimate membership level, offering maximum exposure, VIP access to all events, and premium training resources. This membership is ideal for established businesses aiming for high-impact growth.

Benefits: VIP event invitations, spotlight advertising, and free access to premium workshops.

Technology Access: Featured business listing with homepage promotion.
